Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Vedrix Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Block Blast Frozen mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Leaderboard Name: If you choose to submit your score to the leaderboard, you may provide a display name. This is optional and does not need to be your real name.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and ad delivery purposes.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including game scores, session duration, and feature usage, to improve the App experience.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners may collect data for personalized or non-personalized ads (see Section 3).

1.3 Information Stored Locally

The App stores game progress, high scores, diamond balance, and preferences locally on your device using PlayerPrefs. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you choose to submit a leaderboard score.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Display leaderboard scores
  • Serve advertisements
  • Send push notifications (if you have opted in)
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance gameplay experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the App's core functionality, including gameplay, leaderboards, and in-game economy.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, conduct analytics, prevent fraud, and serve contextual advertisements, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: For personalized advertising, push notifications, and any optional data processing. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve banner and rewarded video advertisements within the App. Google AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how Google handles your data.

4.2 Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand App usage patterns. These services may collect information sent by your device, including usage metrics and device information.

5. Cookies, Ad Identifiers, and Similar Technologies

The App and our website may use the following technologies:

  • Ad Identifiers: Mobile advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) allow our advertising partners to deliver and measure ads. You can reset or limit these identifiers in your device settings.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies -- small text files stored on your device -- to remember preferences and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
  • Analytics SDKs: Third-party SDKs within the App may collect usage data, crash reports, and performance metrics.

6. Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications to inform you about game updates, promotions, or other relevant information. You can opt out of push notifications at any time by adjusting your device settings under the notification preferences for the App.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App (e.g., ad networks, analytics providers), subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of Vedrix Labs, our users, or the public.

8. Data Retention

Locally stored data (game progress, scores, preferences) persists on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data. Leaderboard submissions are retained on our servers indefinitely to maintain the leaderboard. We may delete inactive leaderboard entries periodically.

9. Children's Privacy

Block Blast Frozen is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at vedrixlabs@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

12. Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

13. Do Not Track

Some web browsers transmit "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals to websites. Because there is no universally accepted standard for how to respond to DNT signals, we do not currently respond to them. However, you can manage your ad and tracking preferences as described in the Ad Choices section above.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Vedrix Labs
Email: vedrixlabs@gmail.com

If you are located in the EEA or UK and are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.
